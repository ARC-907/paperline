# drafts-quarantine/

Drafts are explicitly excluded from the corpus. The corpus reflects what was actually sent, not what was considered. This folder is a quarantine area — kept for reference, never used as a record of correspondence.

## What goes here

- Working drafts (markdown, docx) that never got sent
- Office lock files (`~$*.docx`) that were never sent
- Unfinalized templates and branch-response options

## What does NOT go here

- Actually-sent messages (those go to `correspondence/.../<msg>/`)
- Versions of documents that were sent regardless of whether they were signed (those go to `documents/<family>/<version>/`)
- Memos that were actually sent (those go to `memos/from-user/<date>_<slug>/`)
