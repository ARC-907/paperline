# Example Co. data-handling policy — version 2 (revised post-inquiry)

> Placeholder for the actual policy PDF.

## Section 3 — Data retention

Personal data collected by Example Co. is retained for the duration of the customer relationship, plus **18 months** after account closure for the purpose of dispute resolution, audit, and regulatory compliance. (Reduced from 24 months in v1.)

Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for statistical and product-improvement purposes.

**New paragraph added:** Personal data subject to legal hold (active investigation, dispute, or regulatory inquiry) is retained for the duration of the hold regardless of the standard retention period above.

## Section 4 — Data subject rights

Data subjects have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal data on request to data-rights@example-co.example. Requests are processed within **14 days** (reduced from 30 days in v1).

**New paragraph added:** Data subjects also have the right to receive their data in a portable, machine-readable format on request.

## Section 5 — Third-party data sharing

Example Co. shares personal data with the following categories of third parties:

- Payment processors (for transaction processing only)
- Customer support platforms (for ticket management)
- Analytics providers (aggregated and anonymized data only)

**New paragraph added:** A separate processor sub-page lists each named third-party processor with the categories of data shared and the legal basis. Available at example-co.example/processors-detailed.
