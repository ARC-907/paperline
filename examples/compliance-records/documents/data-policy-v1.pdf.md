## Section 3 — Data retention

Personal data collected by Example Co. is retained for the duration of the customer relationship, plus 24 months after account closure for the purpose of dispute resolution, audit, and regulatory compliance.

Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for statistical and product-improvement purposes.

## Section 4 — Data subject rights

Data subjects have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal data on request to data-rights@example-co.example. Requests are processed within 30 days.

## Section 5 — Third-party data sharing

Example Co. shares personal data with the following categories of third parties:

- Payment processors (for transaction processing only)
- Customer support platforms (for ticket management)
- Analytics providers (aggregated and anonymized data only)

A current list of named third-party processors is maintained at example-co.example/processors.
